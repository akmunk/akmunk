I use this space as my research blog and personal website. I am Professor of Computational Anthropology at the Technical University of Denmark where I study  controversies about technology in society and develop new methods to monitor and understand them. I have a mixed background in European Ethnology, Human Geography and STS, but I spend most of my time on methods and the way AI and computational techniques create new possibilities for qualitative analysis at scale. I also play [whale punk](https://open.spotify.com/artist/4ZiE8tzaRquJl7KPoZXNnQ?si=ms6sMrDZS1e0Oipyr5x7SQ) 🐋.

## Full bio
Anders Kristian Munk is Professor of Computational Anthropology and Director of the Observatory for Human-Centred Engineering (ECHOlab) at DTU Management. 

He has previously co-founded and directed the Techno-Anthropology Lab (TANTlab) and MASSHINE (the computational SSH hub) at the University of Aalborg, and he is a co-founder of the Public Data Lab. 

He received his education at the University of Copenhagen (BA, 2004, and Cand.mag., 2006, in European Ethnology) and his doctoral training at Oxford University (D.Phil. in Geography, 2010).
